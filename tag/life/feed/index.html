My journey to becoming a web developer started in my IT lesson, where I was introduced to coding for the first time. From that moment, I was hooked. My first project was a school canteen website, which looking back, was pretty crappy. But it was my first step into the world of web development, and I was determined to keep learning.

As I continued to code, I built a calculator, an aquarium simulator, parlax, a clock, and even a chess clone, These projects helped me hone my skills in programming languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript and react.

Now, I am eager to expand my knowledge in areas like WordPress, marketing, and game engines. I want to be able to build websites that not only look great but also perform well and provide value to users. With WordPress, I can create websites that are easy to manage and update. Marketing will help me promote my skills and services to potential clients. And game engines will enable me to develop engaging and interactive experiences for users.

As I look to the future, I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead. I want to continue learning and growing as a web developer, and I am eager to see where this journey takes me. With the support of the online community and my dedication to improving my skills, I am confident that I can achieve my goals and make a positive impact in the world of web development.
